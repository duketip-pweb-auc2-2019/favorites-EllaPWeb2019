Characters

Victoria-

Alexandrina Victoria is the Queen of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and titular character in ITV's Victoria. She is portrayed by the actress Jenna Coleman in ITV's television series Victoria. She becomes queen at 18 when King William IV dies. After being crowned, she goes on with her life as queen by ruling over her country making some naive decisions, though she keeps a pure and strong heart and perhaps evem finds love.

Albert-

Prince Albert is the husband of Queen Victoria. In the episode Brocket Hall he and his brother come to see their cousin, Victoria, for the first time since she became Queen of England. At that point is when their courtship starts.

Lord Melbourne-

Lord Melbourne (also known as Lord M to Queen Alexandrina Victoria) was the Prime Minister of England and Victoria's private advisor.

Nancy Skerrett-

Nancy is the Queen's Head Dresser at the royalm palace, who is found usually either doing Victoria's hair, or in the kitchen with Mr. Francatelli, the cook.

Baroness Louise Lehzen-

Lehzen was a very good friend of Victoria. They had been with each other from Victoria being as young as five years old when Lehzen became her tutor and Governess. On the day she became Queen, Victoria put the Baroness in charge of her household.

Charles Francatelli-

Charles Francatelli was the Queen's Head Chef. He appears many times in the show in a side story with Miss Skerrett about his feelings for her.

Ernest II-

Ernest is the brother of Prince Albert & cousin of Queen Victoria.

Feodora-

Feodora of Leiningen is Queen Victoria's half-sister. She is portrayed by the actress Kate Fleetwood in ITV's Victoria. Despite the age gap, Victoria and Feodora were said to have been quite close whilst living at Kensington Palace together. Victoria looked up to her elder sister and was devoted to her.
